How to verify encumbrance
  1. Download the Land Encumbrance Certificate (EC) from this report.
  2. Check for terms like mortgage, charge, or a lender name.
  3. Verify whether any charge is marked as active or released.
  4. For unit-specific details, check the RERA portal under Project Progress & Actions.
  5. Also check the Title Report for any pending litigation, court orders, or dispute references that may affect the property title.
Encumbrance status may vary at the unit level. Users are advised to verify details from the official Gujarat RERA portal (gujrera.gujarat.gov.in).
